== Log Parser Studio ==

Overview:

Log Parser Studio is a utility that allows you to search through and create reports from your IIS, Event, EXADB and others types of logs. It builds on top of Log Parser 2.2 and has a full user interface for easy creation and management of related SQL queries. Also includes Export Query to PowerShell functionality.

Version: 2.0.0.100

Requires: LogParser 2.2
